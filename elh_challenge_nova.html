القراءة

اقرئي النص وأجيبي:

Sarah is a 15-year-old student from Alexandria. Last year, she noticed that many elderly people in her neighborhood couldn't order medicine online because they don't know how to use smartphones. She decided to create a simple mobile app called "CareConnect". The app has large buttons and voice commands to help seniors order medicine and book doctor appointments easily. Sarah worked with her IT teacher for six months. Now, over 200 families use her app. She won first place in the National Youth Innovation Competition. Sarah says, "Technology should help everyone, not just young people."

What problem did Sarah want to solve?
